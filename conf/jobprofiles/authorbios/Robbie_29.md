Robbie is an avid collector of folk lore and stories. he has been involved in thr preservation of oral histories since his graduate school days at St. Andrew's University. As a member of the PageKicker team, Robbie has been working on myths, lyrical poetry and folk lore.

Despite rumors to the contrary, Robbie doesn't cook haggis or shortbread. In fact he doesn't cook at all, its just another tall tale Robbie says his Mother spread to find him a "wee lassie."
